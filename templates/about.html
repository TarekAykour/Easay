About Easay

Easay, launched in June 2024, was inspired by the developer's challenges in managing evidence material, feedback, and content from various projects during his second year. Instead of spending time manually organizing everything, he created an app to streamline the process. As of June 24, 2024, Easay can efficiently merge files into one organized document. Future updates will include a file finder, spelling and grammar checks, and machine learning models that predict the user's next input.

Easay is built using Flask, JavaScript, python-docx, and Pillow (PIL). Learn more about these technologies and how Scribbr inspired this innovative project.
